I LOVE SAMOSAS

Ingredients
  • Flour
  • Ghee
  • 6 TBSP Water
  • Carom Seeds
  • Salt
  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Coriander Leaves
Steps
  1. Peel, boil potatoes
  2. Crumble Potatoes
  3. Mix together flour, salt and oil
  4. Add flour until the dough is stiff and pliable
  5. Add spices to potatoes
  6. Add potatoes into flour and bake
